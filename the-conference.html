THE DAY OF THE EVENT - WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW

SNHU is Hosting Business Mindfulness Conference 2019 the two-day event and is run every other month. This collaboration will bring together learn practitioners from Meditation and Mindfulness in Sport, expand Learn knowledge and discuss how in these challenging times we can conquer the obstacles faced on a day to day bases.

The events of the day are geared to reinforce the commitment to Meditation and delivering value and efficiency through learn and Continuous Process Improvement. Participants will hear from an array of speakers, have an opportunity to network with other Learn practitioners and participate in hands-on activities to further enhance their Learn skills.

Be sure to join us at the SNHU Arena in Manchester, NH on the 1st, 2nd of August.
